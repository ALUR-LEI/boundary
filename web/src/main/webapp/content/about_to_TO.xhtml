Using SOLA Community server you can enrol as a Community Member and view land use recordings made by community members. User enrolment can be done online and once you submit your enrolment request the Community Technologist will review it and approve. Upon approval a confirmation code will be sent to your e-mail address. You will then need to activate your enrolment request, by following the received link. Another way is to contact your Community Technologist and they will enroll you as a user.

All Community Member enrolment requests are subject to review and approval by the Community Server Technologist and the Community Moderation Committee.

If you are already registered, use Login page to login into the system.
